# Alternating Current

## Fundamentals (JEE)

### Impedance and Reactance

$$
\begin{gathered}
Z = \frac{V_m}{I_m} = \frac{V_{rms}}{I_{rms}} \\ \\
X_L = \omega L \\ \\
X_C = \frac{1}{\omega C}
\end{gathered}
$$

### Simple AC Circuits

#### AC Circuit with a Resistor only
$$
\begin{gathered}
i_o = \frac{\varepsilon}{R} \\ \\
i = i_o\sin\omega t \\ \\
\varepsilon = \varepsilon_o\sin\omega t
\end{gathered}
$$
